Chicken and Waffles

3-Ingredient Chicken and Waffles from Allrecipes

You only need 3 ingredients to get the full chicken and waffles experience

  • shake and pour pancake mix
  • frozen fully cooked chicken tenders
  • cooking spray
  • maple syrup
  1. Prepare chicken tenders
  2. Prepare pancake mix
  3. heat up waffle iron and spray with cooking spray
  4. dip tenders halfway into batter and place on waffle iron
  5. serve with syrup
