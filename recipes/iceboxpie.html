Icebox Pie

4-Ingredient Icebox Pie from Allrecipes

This pie is the perfect combo of flavors and textures

  • sweetened condensed milk
  • keylime zest or lime zest
  • keylime or lime juice
  • graham cracker sheets
  • powdered sugar
  • vanilla extract
  • crushed graham crackers (optional)
  1. gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk together sweetened condensed milk, lime zest, and lime juice in a medium bowl
  3. Beat the cream in a medium bowl with an electric mixer on medium until soft peaks form (tips curl). Fold whipped cream into the lime mixture
  4. Arrange half of the graham cracker pieces in an even layer in the bottom of a 9-inch pie plate
  5. Spoon half of the filling over the top and gently spread to an even layer
  6. Top with the remaining graham cracker pieces and the remaining filling
  7. Cover and freeze 4 hours or until firm
